## Notes

- **Japanese surveys**: The JPN surveys cover the same metrics as the English versions but were distributed to the Japanese Cardano community. `spo-jpn-survey-translated.csv` is the English translation; `spo-jpn-survey-original.csv` is the original Japanese text.
- **Builder data**: Builder surveys and interviews focus on governance tooling gaps rather than specific numbered metrics.
- **Interview column naming**: Interview CSVs use `-S` suffix in column codes (e.g. `M1-A-S`) to distinguish semi-structured interview questions from the structured survey equivalents.
